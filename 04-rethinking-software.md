# Rethinking How Software is Built in the Age of AI Agents

## Build the Impossible with AI Agents

Build things that were previously impossible to build. AI agents can help you create software that was once out of reach, whether it's a complex application, a new type of user interface, or an innovative solution to a problem.

## Rethinking How We Teach Innovation

Non-technical people can now generate applications in minutes that meet their needs with sophisticated capabilities like image recognition, natural language processing, all customized to their specific requirements. This democratization of software development allows for a broader range of ideas and innovations to flourish, as more people can bring their unique perspectives and creativity to the table.
